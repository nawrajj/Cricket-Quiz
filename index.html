About

Kachankawal Premier League is the best Cricket League of Jhapa which is organized by adarshacricketclub. KPL is organized in every year on the occasion of Dashain or Dipawali. The main goal of this league is to produce good cricketers from Jhapa. Until now we succed to organized KPLseason1 to KPL season3 and continue to organize.

Background

Initially KPL was started playing by Tennis ball which was held on 2075 Asoj 25. Later on we started to play by duce ball.
